Perfectly Chocolate Cake

Description

This moist and simple chocolate cake has become a family favorite. We prefer to bake this cake in a 9x13 glass pan without frosting for a rich, but not too sweet treat to be enjoyed for breakfast or desert. I've adjusted the recipe in accordance to my tastes for more coco powder and less sugar.

  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease and flour 9X13 glass baking pan.
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs, oil, milk and vanilla and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  6. Bake 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool and enjoy at room temperature for best flavor.
