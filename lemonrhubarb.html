Lemon Rhubarb Cookies

Description

Lemon Rhubarb Cookies are soft, melt in your mouth cookies that are sweet and tart with bright flavors of lemon and rhubarb.

  • ¾ c. butter, softened
  • ¾ c. butter, softened
  • ½ c. sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 c. flour
  • 1 Tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ c. rhubarb, chopped
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 Tablespoons lemon juice
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl cream together the butter, brown sugar, and sugar. Add in the egg and mix well.
  3. In a medium bowl combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix well. Your dough may be a little dry at this point.
  5. Stir in the rhubarb, lemon zest, and lemon juice and mix until well combined.
  6. Drop the dough onto un-greased cookie sheets by heaping tablespoons. Place the cookies at least 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the cookies are just starting to brown slightly.
  8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 2 minutes before removing them to a wire rack to cool completely.
